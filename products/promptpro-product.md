## BONUS: PROMPT STACKING TECHNIQUE

Make any prompt 10x more powerful by adding these modifiers:

**Tone modifiers:**
- "Write this in the style of [PERSON/BRAND]"
- "Use a conversational, direct tone — no fluff"
- "Write like you're talking to a smart friend, not a client"

**Output modifiers:**
- "Give me 3 variations"
- "Then critique your own output and improve it"
- "Rate your response out of 10 and explain why"

**Constraint modifiers:**
- "Under 100 words"
- "No jargon, no buzzwords"
- "Start with a question"

**Persona modifiers:**
- "You are an expert [ROLE] with 20 years of experience"
- "Write from the perspective of the customer, not the brand"
- "Write this as if you're the world's best [COPYWRITER/STRATEGIST/COACH]"
